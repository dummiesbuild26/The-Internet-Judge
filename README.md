🔥 The Internet Judges You 🔥

Confess your sins. Receive your verdict. Touch grass accordingly.


What is this?
Honestly? It's a bit of fun.
The Internet Judges You is a silly little web app where you type in something you did — embarrassing, questionable, or just a bit unhinged — and the internet delivers its verdict. You get a score out of 10, a pompous ruling, and the option to appeal to a higher power (spoiler: the appeal is also rejected).
That's it. That's the whole thing.
No AI. No clever algorithm. No deep learning model analysing your soul. Just a random pick from a hand-written list of verdicts, delivered with entirely unearned authority.

Why does this exist?
This is our very first project under Dummies Build — a brand built on the idea that you don't need to be an expert to make something fun. We're learning in public, building in public, and occasionally embarrassing ourselves in public.
We made this because bad days exist, and sometimes you just need something stupid to make you laugh. If this app brightens up even one person's rubbish Tuesday, then it's done its job.

⚠️ Please read this bit
All verdicts are randomly generated and completely made up.
The app does not:

Know anything about you
Read your confession and actually judge it
Use AI to analyse your input
Store what you type anywhere
Have any opinion on you whatsoever

The "judgement" you receive is pulled at random from a pre-written list of funny verdicts. You could type your deepest darkest secret or you could type "banana" — the algorithm does not care. It is equally judgemental to everyone, which is the most fair system we could think of.
We intend absolutely no harm. This is a joke app made for laughs. Please take it in the spirit it was intended — which is chaotic, irreverent, and entirely harmless fun.

🔒 Data & Privacy
We take this seriously even if the app doesn't take itself seriously.

We do not collect your confessions. Nothing you type is stored, sent to a server, or seen by anyone. It lives in your browser and dies when you close the tab.
We do not sell data. We have no data to sell. We are two people with a laptop and a dream.
Analytics: We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand basic traffic — things like how many people visited, what device they used, and which country they're from. Cloudflare does not use cookies or track individuals. We genuinely just want to know if anyone showed up.
No cookies. No tracking pixels. No creepy retargeting ads following you around the internet afterwards.

If you're curious, Cloudflare's privacy policy is here.

🛠️ How it's built
About as simply as possible:

index.html — the entire app. One file. No frameworks, no build tools, no npm install.
judgements.json — the list of verdicts. Just a JSON file.
Hosted on GitHub Pages for free.
Fonts from Google Fonts. Everything else is hand-rolled CSS and vanilla JS.

This is intentional. The whole point of Dummies Build is that you don't need a complicated tech stack to ship something. Sometimes an HTML file and a dream is enough.
